Special Dish

Pepper Chicken Charcoal

Grilled chicken charcoal is a delicious and popular dish that is loved by many. The chicken is marinated in a flavorful mixture of herbs and spices before being cooked on a charcoal grill. The charcoal grill imparts a smoky flavor and a perfectly charred exterior, while the inside remains juicy and tender. Grilled chicken charcoal can be served in a variety of ways, such as as a main dish, as part of a salad, or even in a wrap. It pairs well with a variety of sides, including roasted vegetables, rice, or potatoes.
